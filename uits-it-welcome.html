01 · welcome.txt

A message before orientation begins

To every fresher walking into the IT Department this term — welcome. In a few years you'll stop thinking of "IT" as a course code and start thinking of it as a way you see problems: as things that can be broken down, debugged, and rebuilt better. That shift happens here, in labs, in late-night project crunches, in arguments about tabs vs. spaces you didn't expect to have opinions on.

This department runs on people more than syllabi — seniors who'll show you the shortcut through the registration process, faculty who'll remember your name after one confused question in class, clubs that will absorb every spare hour you're willing to give them. Orientation week is just the install process. Everything after is the real program running.

We're genuinely glad you're here. Let's begin.

— Department of Information Technology, UITS
On behalf of faculty, seniors & orientation committee
